Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 22, 2026 — Last updated: June 22, 2026

FocusBro ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use FocusBro at focusbro.net, how we use that information, who we share it with, and the rights you have over your personal data. FocusBro is a browser-based productivity toolkit designed for people with ADHD, and privacy-first design is central to everything we build.

By using FocusBro you agree to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the service. If you have any questions, you can always reach us at support@focusbro.net.

1. Information We Collect

a. Data You Store Locally (Browser Storage)

FocusBro is designed with a local-first philosophy. The vast majority of your data — Pomodoro session history, task lists, focus goals, energy tracker entries, timer settings, intentions, notes, and preferences — is stored directly in your browser using localStorage and sessionStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable a cloud sync feature. You can delete all locally stored FocusBro data at any time via your browser's privacy or site settings (typically found under Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data → Site settings).

b. Optional Account Data (Cloud Sync)

If you create an optional account or enable cloud synchronization features, we may collect your email address, a chosen display name, and an authentication credential (either a hashed password or an OAuth token from a third-party provider like Google). This data is used solely to authenticate you and to synchronize your app state across your own devices. We do not sell, rent, or share this data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

c. Technical and Operational Logs

Our hosting and content delivery infrastructure — provided by Cloudflare — automatically captures standard server access logs. These include your IP address, the browser and device type you are using, the URLs you requested, the date and time of requests, and the HTTP status codes returned. These logs are used for security monitoring, abuse prevention, and performance diagnostics. Cloudflare retains these logs according to their own data retention schedule; see cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.

d. Advertising and Analytics Data

We display advertisements through Google AdSense and measure aggregate site usage through Google Analytics. Both services place cookies and use device identifiers to function. These are third-party services and their data collection is governed by Google's own privacy policies, described in detail in Sections 3 and 4 below.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Powering app features: Your locally stored data is used exclusively to deliver the functionality you expect — saving tasks, tracking focus sessions, remembering settings between visits.
  • Security and reliability: Technical logs help us detect and respond to abuse, diagnose errors, and maintain service availability.
  • Displaying advertisements: Google AdSense uses cookies to serve relevant ads. Revenue from advertising allows us to keep FocusBro entirely free for users.
  • Improving the product: Aggregated, anonymized analytics data from Google Analytics helps us understand which tools users find most valuable and where we should focus our development efforts.
  • Account management: If you create an account, your email is used to authenticate you and send transactional messages (password resets, sync notifications).

3. Google AdSense and Advertising Cookies

FocusBro participates in the Google AdSense program, an advertising service operated by Google LLC (and its subsidiaries). When advertisements are displayed on FocusBro, Google and its advertising partners may use the following types of tracking technologies:

  • HTTP cookies placed in your browser
  • Web beacons (1×1 pixel images) embedded in ad content
  • Device fingerprinting signals such as browser version, screen resolution, and installed fonts
  • Unique advertising identifiers (Google Advertising ID on Android, Identifier for Advertisers on iOS)

These technologies allow Google and its partners to: serve personalized advertisements based on your interests and browsing history; limit the number of times you see the same advertisement; measure whether you viewed or clicked on an ad; detect and prevent fraudulent or invalid ad traffic; and attribute conversions (purchases, sign-ups) to ad campaigns.

The DoubleClick Cookie

Google uses the DoubleClick cookie (now part of the Google Marketing Platform, served from doubleclick.net) to uniquely identify your browser across Google's advertising network. The DoubleClick cookie stores a pseudonymous identifier that allows Google to match ad impressions, clicks, and conversions across many websites and apps. This identifier may be combined with other Google account data if you are signed into a Google account while browsing FocusBro.

How to Opt Out of Personalized Advertising

You have several options to limit or opt out of interest-based advertising from Google:

  • Visit Google Ads Settings at google.com/settings/ads to review and adjust your ad personalization preferences.
  • Use the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out tool at optout.networkadvertising.org to opt out of interest-based ads from multiple networks simultaneously.
  • Use the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out tool at optout.aboutads.info.
  • Install the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-On from tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.
  • Use FocusBro's cookie consent banner (shown on first visit) to decline advertising cookies.

For comprehensive information about how Google uses data in connection with its advertising services, please visit: google.com/policies/technologies/ads.

4. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand aggregate usage patterns on FocusBro. Google Analytics uses first-party cookies — primarily _ga (2-year expiry), _gid (24-hour expiry), and _gat (1-minute expiry) — as well as device and browser identifiers to collect data including: pages viewed, session duration, bounce rate, device category (desktop/mobile/tablet), geographic region (country and city level), and traffic source (how you arrived at FocusBro).

This data is collected and processed by Google LLC. We do not share raw Analytics data with any third party outside of Google's standard data processing. We have configured GA4 with data retention set to 14 months. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-On.

5. Types of Cookies We Use

A cookie is a small text file stored in your browser that allows websites to recognize your device across page loads. Below is a summary of cookie categories used on FocusBro:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Essential for the website to function. They include session state identifiers and your cookie consent choice (cookieConsent). These cannot be disabled without breaking core functionality.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences such as UI theme, timer defaults, and tool view selection. Not required for core functionality but improve your experience.
  • Analytics Cookies: Set by Google Analytics to collect anonymized, aggregate usage statistics. Help us understand which features are most useful. Examples: _ga, _gid, _gat_UA-*.
  • Advertising Cookies: Set by Google AdSense and associated advertising partners to deliver personalized or contextual ads and measure ad effectiveness. Examples: IDE (DoubleClick), test_cookie, DSID.

On your first visit to FocusBro, a cookie consent banner appears at the bottom of the screen. Clicking Accept consents to all cookie categories. Clicking Decline opts you out of advertising and analytics cookies while keeping strictly necessary cookies. You can withdraw consent at any time by clearing cookies for focusbro.net in your browser settings.

6. Data Retention

  • Browser localStorage: Persists until you manually clear site data or uninstall your browser. No server-side copy exists unless cloud sync is enabled.
  • Server access logs (Cloudflare): Retained for up to 90 days.
  • Google Analytics data: Retained for 14 months per our GA4 settings, after which it is automatically deleted.
  • Account data (if applicable): Retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights — GDPR (EU / EEA / UK Residents)

If you are located in the European Union, European Economic Area, or United Kingdom, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable national data protection laws grant you the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Ask us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Request deletion of your personal data where we have no legitimate reason to continue processing it.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive a structured, commonly used, machine-readable copy of the personal data you have provided to us.
  • Right to Restriction of Processing: Ask us to limit how we use your personal data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your personal data where we rely on legitimate interests, including objecting to direct marketing.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on your consent, withdraw that consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, please email support@focusbro.net with the subject line "GDPR Data Request." We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your national supervisory authority (e.g., the ICO in the UK, or your EU member state's data protection authority).

8. Your Rights — CCPA (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) grants you the following rights:

  • Right to Know: Request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell about you in the preceding 12 months.
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale or Sharing: We do not sell personal data in the traditional sense. However, sharing data with Google AdSense for targeted advertising may constitute a "sale" or "sharing" under CCPA. You may opt out by declining advertising cookies via our consent banner or by adjusting your Google Ad Settings.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not treat you differently for exercising your CCPA rights.

To submit a CCPA request, please email support@focusbro.net with the subject line "CCPA Request."

9. Children's Privacy

FocusBro is not directed at or intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EU/EEA, where applicable). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under these ages. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us immediately at support@focusbro.net. Upon verification, we will take prompt steps to delete that information from our systems.
